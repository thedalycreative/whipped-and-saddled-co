Principal Partner

PRINCIPAL PARTNER EQUINE THERAPY WA WHEATBELT

Wheatbelt Equine Therapies

Our proudest partnership — and, we should stress, an entirely wholesome one. Wheatbelt Equine Therapies is the WA Wheatbelt's trusted provider of professional equine therapy services: horse massage, rehabilitation, musculoskeletal therapy, and equine wellbeing care for performance horses, working horses and beloved paddock companions across the Wheatbelt region of Western Australia.

Whether your horse needs post-race recovery, injury rehabilitation, or simply the kind of deep-tissue attention that keeps a hard-working animal sound and happy, their qualified equine therapists deliver genuinely world-class results. After a demanding session with our gear — training gear, obviously — there is no one we'd rather send a horse to.

Searching for "equine therapy Wheatbelt WA", "horse massage Western Australia" or "equine rehabilitation near Northam"? This is who you're looking for. Tell them the family at Whipped Saddled & Co. sent you — they'll know what it means. (It means horses. It means horses.)
