About Belmont Horse Floggers

Based in Belmont, Western Australia, we've been hand-crafting quality leather floggers and equestrian gear for local riders, trainers, and stockyards since day one. Every flogger is cut, oiled, and stitched by hand using genuine leather sourced from trusted Australian tanneries.

Whether you're after a reliable everyday piece or a custom-built flogger to your exact specification, our small team takes pride in gear that's made to work as hard as you do.
