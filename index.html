Our Stable, Our Story

Whipped Saddled & Co. serves Aussie locals with horses in their lives — in whatever capacity that may be.

Whether you own them, train them up, ride them, groom them or simply work with them; whether you're a discrete farmer or a young up-and-coming saddler — you'll get everything you want and need when you come to us. And you will come to us, because nobody else in the West does leather like we do.

Premium and excellence aren't buzzwords here — they're the brand. Full-grain Australian leather, marine-grade stainless steel, and stitching tested well beyond anything a horse could reasonably do to it. We build gear for hard riders, long sessions and demanding stallions, and we stand behind every piece with our 13-Year Riders Promise: if it fails you in the saddle within thirteen years, we'll replace it, no questions asked. (We've learned not to ask questions.)
